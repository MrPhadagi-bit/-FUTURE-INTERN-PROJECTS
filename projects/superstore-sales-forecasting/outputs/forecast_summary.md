# Sales Forecast Summary

## What the forecast means

- The dataset covers 2015-01-01 to 2018-12-01 across 48 monthly periods.
- After cleaning, the model used 9,994 valid transactions from 4 regions and 3 product categories.
- On the final holdout window, the forecasting model reached an MAE of $17,657, RMSE of $20,541, and MAPE of 20.8%.
- The model outperformed a seasonal naive baseline, which had an MAE of $20,460.
- The next 6 months are forecast at a total of $340,964, with an average month 28.4% below the latest six-month average.
- The strongest forecast month is March 2019 at $66,127.
- The softest forecast month is February 2019 at $43,312.

## How a business can use this

- Inventory teams can stock more aggressively before March and stay leaner near February.
- Finance teams can use the projected revenue range to plan cash flow and purchasing schedules.
- Staffing managers can align promotions, labor, and fulfillment capacity to expected demand instead of reacting late.
- Seasonality appears strongest in November, so campaigns and replenishment plans should be locked in ahead of that month.
